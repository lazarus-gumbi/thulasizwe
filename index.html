Who are we?

TSMS is the brainchild of veteran broadcaster and journalist, Thulasizwe Simelane. With it, Thulasizwe brings to bear his 21 years of experience in the media industry, allowing you to tap into his writing, editing, TV and Radio broadcasting skills and talents for your story-telling needs.


That's the reason behind our brand promise; 'Your Story, Our Canvas, Your Masterpiece'.


Give us your story, let's put it on our canvas and work our magic on it, and give it back to you as a masterpiece you can share proudly with your audiences, achieving your communication goals.
